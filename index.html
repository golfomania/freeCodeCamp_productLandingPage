beginning

Marthen Bishop presented his idea of an illuminated cat in one of his viedos . Body and Head of the cat where printed on an 3D printing machine. Inside the cat a Wemos d1 mini (an esp8266 board) microcontroller powered by USB is lighting up an WS2812b RGB LED.

build

I wanted to make more out of this idea. So I decided to build a voice controlled cat. Therefore I selected Alexa as voice control service and bring the ESP microcontroller to listen to the commands of alexa.
